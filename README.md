## What is Kindled?
 
Kindled is an annual reading story that transforms Kindle's behavioral data into a personal narrative. Every November, readers unlock a 10-slide, swipeable experience that reveals who they are as readers such as their personality type, reading rhythm, genre taste, highlight patterns, and more. The core insight: Kindle already logs everything readers do. The opportunity is to give it back to them in a form they actually want to see and share.
 
Think Spotify Wrapped, but for reading.

## Context & Inspiration

This concept was developed as a product thinking exercise. The insight: Kindle has the richest behavioral dataset in reading history. The gap: it has never given readers a way to see their year reflected back to them. The parallel: Spotify Wrapped showed that annual recaps drive retention, word-of-mouth, and cultural moments when done right.
